A Gift of School Supplies

>Education Program | 2021

About the Initiative

In 2021-2022, a program was launched to provide educational materials to aspiring students from single-parent homes, orphans, and financially weak families. This initiative, supported by Veer Foundation, Hadapsar, reached students at Rayat Shikshan Sanstha's schools in Davdi and Wafgaon, ensuring access to vital learning resources.


In 2024 ,At Rayat Shikshan Sanstha's Shri. M.Y.Holkar Vidyalaya, Wafgaon, distributed essential educational materials (notebooks, books, compasses) to 25-30 hostel students. This impactful support was made possible through the generous financial assistance of Hon. Pandey Madam from Pune.
