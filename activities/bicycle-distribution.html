Bicycle Distribution for Girls

Bicycle Distribution | 2024

About the Initiative

Imagine a young girl, her day beginning with a long, exhausting walk to school under the hot sun. This daily struggle not only saps her energy but often discourages her from pursuing her education altogether.

In March 2024, with the generous help of Hon. Prof. Vidula Madam, we launched a bicycle distribution program at Jawahar Vidyalaya Chas, Tal Khed, Dist. Pune. For these 10 girls, the bicycle wasn't just a vehicle; it was a key to a new future.


Program Impact

It shortened their travel time, made their journey safer, and gave them the freedom and confidence to pursue their education without the burden of a long, tiring commute.
