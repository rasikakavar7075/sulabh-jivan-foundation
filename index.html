About Sulabh Jeevan Foundation

Dedicated to empowering rural youth, women, and girls since 2018

A small step can change a life. Since 2018, Sulabh Jivan Foundation has worked to empower rural youth, women, and girls. We believe in creating a world where every individual can be self-reliant and achieve their full potential. Join us in building a progressive society, one opportunity at a time.

Explore Our Core Programs

1. Education

Empowering both girls and boys in rural areas with access to education, resources to unlock their full potential.

2. Women Empowerment

Providing health, legal, and entrepreneurial support to foster women's independence and self-reliance.

3. Health and Nutrition

Funding health awareness sessions, tackling malnutrition, and supporting programs like 'Nutri-Kitchen' for community well-being and vitality.

4. Environment and New Jobs

Fostering sustainable practices, promoting eco-friendly products (like cloth bags), and providing crucial entrepreneurial training for women's independence.
